Why are snaps good for Go projects?

Go doesn't offer a method for delivering app updates to end users, and you're on your own when it comes to showcasing your project. Snaps offer a way to fill these gaps.
  • Snaps are easy to discover and install, with millions installing from the Snap Store every day.
  • They auto-update, ensuring users always have the latest stable version of your app.
  • And they're reliable, rolling back on failure while preserving your app's versioned data.
